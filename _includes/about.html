Meet Toby

STRENGTH SKILL STRETCH

  • 2014 - 2018

    University of Bath

    While I was studying at university of Bath I started to take my training much more seriously and regularly helped out at the gym. I mostly focused on weights and learned the best ways to increase my strength and fitness.

  • 2018 - 2019

    Qualified Personal Trainer

    After graduating, I qualified as a personal trainer full time and started working with clients in Shrewsbury and at local schools. I really enjoyed helping clients achieve their fitness goals. Personally, I made the switch to calisthenics (bodyweight movements) to further gain strength and control of my body. I then passed on my learnings to my clients to help them progress further.

  • 2019 - 2020

    Transition to Hybrid Training

    Later, I transitioned into a hybrid approach of weighted and bodyweight movements after discovering this had the best success with not only myself but also with my clients. Through these years, I expanded my learning about flexibility. I understood the importance of keeping mobile in order to maintaining fit and healthy.

  • 2021 - Present

    Phase Two Expansion

    After 7 plus years in the fitness industry, I have managed to expose myself to several different types of training. This allows me to adapt my approach to every new client I work with and tailor it to best suit their goals.
